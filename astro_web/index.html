Astro is a fast-paced game where players cash out earnings as a rocket’s value climbs from 1x to 100x—before it crashes randomly. Thrilling risk & reward!

ROADMAP

Oficial launch: November 2024

Lite Paper & Tokenomics: November 2024

DEX Listing: December 2024


Key Features:

40% of the Tokens will be allocated for Farming

Dynamic Cash-Out Mechanism: Players can cash out their earnings at any point before the rocket crashes.
Gamification and User Engagement: Users can invite friends to join the game, earning rewards for each new player they bring in.
Leaderboards and achievements foster a competitive environment, enhancing user retention.
Farming System: Every six hours, players receive tokens and play tickets, providing a continuous incentive to engage with the game.
